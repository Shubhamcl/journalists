## Why this fits the site

Age verification is turning policy into a normal part of using apps and websites. It affects privacy, identity, access, and the everyday experience of the internet.

## Why now

Regulators and lawmakers are increasing pressure on online services to identify children and restrict access to age-sensitive features or content.
