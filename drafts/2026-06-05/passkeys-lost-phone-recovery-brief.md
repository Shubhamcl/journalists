## Why this fits the site

Passkeys are replacing passwords across major platforms, but fear of losing access remains one of the biggest barriers for ordinary users.

## Why now

More websites and apps are offering or preferring passkeys, while the recovery model still feels unfamiliar compared with resetting a password.
