# About Page Positioning

Date: 2026-06-02

## Decision

The Tech With Life About page should read as an editorial manifesto, not a corporate profile.

The page title is:

```text
Technology Is Becoming the Shape of Life
```

The page excerpt is:

```text
What happens when the tools we use become the world we live inside?
```

The central editorial idea is that technology is no longer separate from daily life. It is becoming part of the architecture of how people work, learn, create, connect, remember, trust, spend attention, and understand the world.

## Tone

The language should feel thoughtful, human, and meaning-led. Avoid startup language such as:

- mission
- value proposition
- audience
- coverage areas
- editorial buckets

Reader-facing copy should convey a worldview rather than a business plan.

## Core Line

```text
Technology no longer waits at the edge of life.
```

## Practical Impact

Future About page edits should preserve the idea that Tech With Life covers technology where it touches ordinary human life, not merely as an industry of products, companies, and announcements.
